Mac and Cheese

How to make mac and cheese

Mac and cheese is proof that food doesn't need to be extravagant and complex in order to taste amazing. This recipe does exactly that, and only requires a few ingredients besides the macaroni.

This recipe serves 2 people

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Macaroni
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of cheddar cheese (but lets be serious, you can't have too much cheese)

Steps

  1. First, get the macaroni to a boil on the stove for around 10 minutes.
  2. Begin the roux making part by melting some butter in a pot on the stove. Not too high a heat as we don't want it burning.
  3. Gradually add flower bit by bit until the roux turns into a thick brownish paste.
  4. Now, pour the milk bit-by-bit ensuring there are no lumps in the sauce. I recommend adding some salt, pepper, and paprika at this point. Let the sauce simmer for 2-5 minutes.
  5. Somewhere around this point, your macaroni should be finished cooking. If your sauce is done, drain the macaroni and pour it into the sauce.
  6. Now add as much cheddar as you like into the macaroni and let it all melt together.
  7. And that's it! The great thing about Mac and Cheese it's highly customizable. If you want to, you can add differernt types of cheese, bacon, bake it, whatever comes to mind!
